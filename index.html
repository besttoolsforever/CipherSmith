    The Perfect Balance of Strong and Simple.

    This is NOT a password manager.

    CipherSmith generates passwords and does not store, save, or manage them in any way. Once you navigate away, the generated password is gone forever. All generation happens locally in your browser for maximum privacy. We use `window.crypto` for cryptographically secure random generation.
